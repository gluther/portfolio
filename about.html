Years Creating

Photography

My journey with photography began as a way to document travels and has evolved into a passion for storytelling through images. I specialize in landscape photography, but have a newfound appreciation for urban photography after moving to New York City. I grew up shooting with a Canon EOS 60D but now use a Ricoh GR III.

Design

As a designer, I've worked with organizations like Major League Soccer, LIV Golf, and Gazelle Group, creating visual identities and marketing materials that communicate effectively and resonate with audiences. I believe in clean, purposeful design that serves both form and function.

Approach

Whether behind a camera or a computer, my approach is rooted in observation, experimentation, and attention to detail. I'm constantly learning, exploring new techniques, and pushing creative boundaries to deliver work that stands out.
